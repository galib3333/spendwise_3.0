Privacy by Design

Most expense trackers require accounts and upload your financial data to their servers. SpendWise is different.

  • No account required — just open and start tracking
  • All data stored locally on your device
  • No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry
  • Optional PIN lock with AES-256 encryption
  • Works 100% offline
  • Open source — verify the code yourself
